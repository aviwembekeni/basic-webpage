Historical Places in Cape Town

    District Six
  • District Six was named the Sixth Municipal District of Cape Town in 1867.
  • It was established as a mixed community.
  • District Six was a vibrant centre with close links to the city and the port
    Bo Kaap
  • The earliest development in the Bo Kaap area was undertaken in the 1760s.
  • The area became known as Waalendorp.
  • During apartheid, the Bo Kaap was subject to Group Areas Act.
    Good Hope Castle
  • Good Hope Castle was built between 1666 and 1679.
  • Its position marks the original shoreline.
  • It’s the oldest existing colonial building in South Africa.
